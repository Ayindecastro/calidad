About Us

CE was conceived by DJ AV in 2008 with the intent of creating an entertainment company were clients could hire artists that brought the best talent and a minimum of 10 years experience in their respective fields. Calidad means quality in Spanish. Our motto is, “Always provide the client quality over quantity.” Our goal is to provide clients with the highest quality service when it comes to:

  • DJ and VJ Services
  • Master of Ceremony Services (English or Spanish)
  • Event Planning
  • Event Sound
  • Event Microphones
  • Event Lighting
  • Event Videography
  • HD Screen and Projector Rentals
  • Graphic/Flyer Design
  • Playlist Creation and Maintenance

Meet The Artists

Below are Calidad Entertainment's artists

DJ AV

DJ

AV is the founder and head of CE. AV has been involved in the nightlife world for 21 years. DJ is passionate about music and culture. He enjoys providing clients with the highest quality service. AV became a professional DJ during his years at The University of Texas at Austin. AV held down a radio show at the campus station 91.7 FM KVRX, residencies at various clubs and parties on 6th street and DJ’d numerous events for student groups. Upon graduation in 2002, AV worked at Telemundo KXTX 39 for 2 years as a production assistant and audio engineer. In 2004, AV embarked on a 4 year enlistment in the United States Army where he served as a Finance Specialist. AV’s tours included Ft. Jackson, South Carolina, Ft. Riley, Kansas and Camp Casey, The Republic of Korea. AV continued to hone his DJ and music skills during this time. AV started an MS in Accounting at The University of Texas at Dallas after separating from the Army in 2008. In 2009 AV began hosting and promoting his own events with DJ Select. AVs most recent accomplishment includes having a DJ cameo in NBC DFWs “Up Here” commercial which featured Mark Cuban, the NBC reporting team and various North Texas locals and aired during the 2016 Olympics.
